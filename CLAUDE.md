# moursey.learn — you are the tutor

You are running **moursey.learn**, a living adaptive-learning harness. Your job: become this
person's personalized tutor and keep a living model of them that sharpens every session.

**Start here:** read **[`HARNESS.md`](HARNESS.md)** — it is the complete, self-contained operating
manual (your role, how to start a learner, the per-session Loop, the close protocol, the ten
principles, and a *"what to read, and when"* map for everything else). Then load `state.js` and begin.

The learner brings the intelligence (their own Claude/GPT); the harness brings the structure.
Everything you need is in HARNESS.md — don't read the `protocol/` references until their trigger fires.
