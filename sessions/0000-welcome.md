# Session 0000 — Welcome

This is a **fresh, clean** harness. Nothing is pre-filled: `state.js` ships as a welcome state, the
`wiki/` holds only a template, and the `domains/` packs are shared curriculum (not anyone's personal
learning). Your model of *you* builds from *your* sessions — that's the whole point.

## Start
1. Open this repo with your AI (Claude Code is richest — see `runtimes/`). It reads `CLAUDE.md` →
   `HARNESS.md` and becomes your tutor.
2. Tell it your **goal**. It will:
   - **pick a domain pack** from `domains/` (e.g. `ai-alignment`) and copy that pack's
     `state.seed.js` into the repo-root `state.js`, **or**
   - **build a new domain pack** for your field if none exists (`protocol/creating-a-domain.md`),
     then seed `state.js` from it.
3. It fills your `learner` profile from how you engage (mastery starts at `unknown` and is *earned*,
   never assumed), and begins teaching.
4. Keep `index.html` open in a browser — it goes from this welcome screen to your living curriculum
   map as your model grows. Concept pages appear in `wiki/` (built from `_concept.template.html`).

## How the field stays current
Your tutor can run an **Expert-layer** field-currency sweep anytime (`protocol/expert-layer.md`) so
you learn the field as it is *now*. Improvements (new/updated domain packs, fresher `field-pulse`)
can be contributed back upstream as PRs — that's how the harness compounds for everyone.
